



\section{Methodology}

The study begins with the formulation of the coupled PDE system within a continuum mechanics framework.

Numerical methods are employed to approximate the solution, with particular attention to handling 
the nonlinear coupling between mechanics and electrical conduction.

In addition, hybrid approaches are explored, where the constitutive relation $\sigma(\varepsilon)$ 
is approximated using data-driven models.

The methodology aims to balance physical accuracy with computational efficiency.
