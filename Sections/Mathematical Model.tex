\section{Mathematical Model}

The coupled electromechanical system is described by

\begin{equation}
    \nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{\sigma}(u) = 0,
\end{equation}


\begin{equation}
    \nabla \cdot \left( \sigma(\varepsilon(u)) \nabla V \right) = 0,
\end{equation}

where $u$ is the displacement field and $V$ is the electrical potential.

This system captures the interaction between elasticity and electrical conduction 
in a continuum setting.
